Dad's Pancakes

Description

A Cagan family classic. Pancake mix can be made by pre-mixing all dry ingredients to simplify preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup corn meal
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
    • <1i>1 tsp salt
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp butter/oil
  • 1-1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1-2 eggs

Steps

  1. Sift flours twice
  2. Mix salt, sugar, baking powder, butter, and milk together, with eggs mixed in last.
  3. Add flours and mix until just combined, do not ovemrmix!
  4. Fry in oil
